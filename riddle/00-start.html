In this game, you will have to solve the riddles given.

But it's not just testing your riddling skills!

This also involves your knowledge in computer science, math, and your general knowledge. It may be requiring other skills, who knows?

The way you enter your answer... is a weird one. You have to change the link you're accessing to https://lithonhong.github.io/riddle/[level number]-[answer].html. If it is a single digit, add a 0 in front.

For example, if you think the answer to level 17 is "fish", you should change the name of your link to https://lithonhong.github.io/riddle/17-fish.html

The answers are sometimes a single word, sometimes a string of letters or numbers. Sometimes it can be more than one word. Remove any spaces from answers and remove any capitalisation (answers in lowercase only).
